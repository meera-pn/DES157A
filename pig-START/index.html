Game of PIG

The player whose turn it is rolls the dice. The total of the roll is added to the current player's score, unless either die comes up as a "one." If this happens, this player's turn is over, and it is the other player’s turn.

If the current player feels their luck is running thin, they can pass to the other player. The first player to reach 30 points wins.

If you roll two "ones" (snake eyes), your current score gets zeroed out. So don’t do that!
